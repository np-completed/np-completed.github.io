    Graph

    graph and flowchart are interchangeable mermaid flowcharts

    graph TD;
    A-->B;
    A-->C;
    B-->D;
    C-->D;

    Use HTML with mermaid

    graph TD; A-->B; A-->C; B-->D; C-->D;

    Sequence Diagram

    sequenceDiagram
    participant Alice
    participant Bob
    Alice->>John: Hello John, how are you?
    loop Healthcheck
        John->>John: Fight against hypochondria
    end
    Note right of John: Rational thoughts <br/>prevail!
    John-->>Alice: Great!
    John->>Bob: How about you?
    Bob-->>John: Jolly good!
